Create a new cinematic interactive storytelling section titled:

"رحلة الحديد عبر الجزائر"

This section should represent the physical journey of iron ore from the Gara Djebilet mine in southern Algeria to the northern Mediterranean industrial and export hubs.

GENERAL CONCEPT

Display a large stylized map of Algeria centered on the screen.

The map should have a dark satellite-style appearance with subtle desert textures.

Overlay the railway route connecting the following cities:

Gara Djebilet → Tindouf → Bechar → Mecheria → Oran

The railway should appear as a glowing neon-green path representing the mining railway corridor.

TRAIN JOURNEY

Add an animated freight train traveling along the railway path.

The train movement must be synchronized with the user's scroll progress.

Scroll progression behavior:

0% scroll → train at Gara Djebilet
25% scroll → train reaches Tindouf
50% scroll → train reaches Bechar
75% scroll → train reaches Mecheria
100% scroll → train reaches Oran

CITY NODES

Each city should be represented by a glowing circular node.

When the train reaches a city:

• the node lights up with a pulse animation
• a small information card appears near the city
• a soft glow spreads along the railway

Tindouf
Strategic mining hub and processing center connecting the mine to national transport networks.

Bechar
Major logistical node in the western mining railway corridor.

Mecheria
Emerging industrial zone for iron transformation and steel production.

Oran
Mediterranean gateway for industry, steel production and export.
